# Telecom-Company-Customer-Churn-Analysis-EDA-
Data analysis project exploring telecom customer churn using Python to uncover key factors affecting customer retention through exploratory data analysis and visualizations.



Telecom Customer Churn Analysis
 Project Overview

Customer churn is a major challenge in the telecom industry. Companies lose significant revenue when customers discontinue their services. This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a telecom customer dataset to identify patterns and factors that contribute to customer churn.

The goal of this analysis is to understand customer behavior and extract actionable insights that can help telecom companies improve customer retention strategies.

 Objectives:

Analyze customer demographics and service usage.

Identify key factors influencing customer churn.

Understand patterns in contracts, payment methods, and service usage.

Provide business insights and recommendations to reduce churn.

 Dataset Description

The dataset contains 7043 customer records and 38 features related to telecom services.

Important Features

Customer Information: Gender, Age, Senior Citizen

Service Details: Internet Service, Phone Service, Streaming Services

Account Information: Contract Type, Payment Method, Tenure

Billing Details: Monthly Charges, Total Revenue

Customer Status: Stayed, Churned, Joined

Churn Details: Churn Category, Churn Reason

 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The following analyses were performed:

1️ Customer Status Analysis

Examined distribution of Stayed vs Churned vs Joined customers.

2️ Tenure Analysis

Analyzed relationship between customer tenure and churn rate.

3️ Contract Type Analysis

Compared churn rates across:

Month-to-Month

One-Year

Two-Year contracts.

4️ Internet Service Analysis

Investigated churn behavior for different internet services.

5️ Monthly Charges Analysis

Studied how billing amount affects churn probability.

6️ Payment Method Analysis

Analyzed how different payment methods influence customer retention.

7️ Churn Category & Reason

Identified major reasons customers leave the service.

 Key Insights

Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate.

Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn.

Customers using fiber optic internet services tend to churn more.

Electronic check payment method shows higher churn compared to automatic payment methods.

A significant portion of customers churn due to better competitor offers.

 Business Recommendations

Provide incentives for customers to switch to long-term contracts.

Improve service quality and network reliability.

Introduce loyalty programs for long-term customers.

Offer competitive pricing plans to retain customers.

Enhance customer support and service experience.
